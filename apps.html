Open Source Apps

Tools I wished existed, so I built them and gave them away — free, open source, and fully offline. Each one started as a real-world problem: I designed the solution, planned what it needed to do, and carried it from idea to a published, privacy-respecting app.

Martial Body

on F-Droid

A free, offline 24-week MMA training programme for complete beginners.

Most fitness apps dump you into an exercise library and leave you guessing — and the open-source ecosystem had no structured combat-sports programme at all. Martial Body takes you from zero fitness to walk-in-ready for your first MMA class across four phases and 24 weeks: guided sessions, built-in timers, automatic deload weeks, progress analytics. No accounts, no ads, no cloud — it can't even access the internet.

24-week programme 100% offline GPL-3.0 Android

WarrantyBoxx

F-Droid · coming soon

Every warranty. Always remembered.

People lose money every year on products that were still under warranty — receipts get lost, expiry dates are forgotten. WarrantyBoxx turns the moment of purchase into a 60-second entry: track warranties, subscriptions, and renewal dates, attach receipt photos, and get reminded before anything expires. A claim assistant helps you gather what a successful claim needs. Fully offline — no account, no server, no tracking.
